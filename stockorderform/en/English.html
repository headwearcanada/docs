How to use the
Stock Order Form

Enter Your Information

Before you begin entering the styles you want to order, use the spaces provided to add your contact information, like company name, account number (if you know it), and contact information.

Don’t forget your Purchase Order number (PO#)!

Add a Style Number

In the first space beneath the “STYLE#” header, type the four-digit style number you want to order.

Don’t know what styles we have available?

Download our Stock Catalog

Select a Product Colour Way

The order form document is programmed to only allow you to select a colour way that is available with the style number you entered.

For example, you can’t select Leaf Print Camouflage for a 4243 Acrylic Beanie because 4243 isn’t available in that colour way.

The Stock Order document does not account for styles and/or colour ways that are out of stock. Just because you can add it to the order form does not guarantee stock availability.

Enter the Product Size

If it is applicable to the style number you entered, you must select an option from the “SIZE” column.

When you enter a style number that requires you to select a size, it should automatically highlight the cell until you select one.

If a selection is not made on applicable styles in your order, your order will be held back until we can contact you regarding which size you would like to order.

The Stock Order document does not account for sizes that are out of stock. Just because you can add it to the order form does not guarantee stock availability.

Enter the Quantity to Order

In the “QTY” column, enter the quantity of that style & colour way you want to order.

You will have noticed that when you added the style number in step 1, the unit cost of the product showed up automatically in the “COST” column.

When you enter the quantity you want to order, the form will automatically display the subtotal for that style & colour way in the “SUBTOTAL” column by multiplying the unit cost by the quantity.

Repeat steps 1 through 4 for all the styles and colour ways you want to order.

Please note the amount in the “SUBTOTAL” column does not include applicable taxes. Taxes will be calculated and applied when the invoice for your order is being created.

Enter the Delivery Address

Regardless of whether this is your first order or hundredth, it is important that we know where to send your order.

If your shipping information is left empty, your order will be sent to the address on file or we may have to hold your order until we can reach out and ask you for a ship-to address.

Add Payment Info

If you’re not sure whether we have your credit card on file or you have NET 30-Day Terms with us, it’s a good idea to add your payment info to the form so there’s no delaying your order.

If your form is missing payment info and your account doesn't have NET 30-Day Terms, your order will be help until we receive your payment information on our Credit Card Authorization form via email.

That’s It!

Now that you’ve filled out the order form, you can submit it via email.

Submit your order via email

If you still have questions or are having trouble with the form, please contact our office by email or by phone and we’d be more than happy to help you out with it!
